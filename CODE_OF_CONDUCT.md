# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free collaboration environment.

Examples of expected behavior:

- being respectful in disagreement
- assuming good intent while giving clear feedback
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- helping others learn and succeed
- respecting privacy and security boundaries

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insulting language
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- disruptive or bad-faith conduct
- knowingly introducing malicious code or security abuse

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers.
